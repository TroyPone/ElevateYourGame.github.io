About

Elevate Your Game, LLC is a services company specializing in basketball training for youth athletes. My primary goals are aimed at developing fundamental basketball skills while having fun and building self-confidence. Elevate Your Game was started because I have a passion for the sport of basketball and love working with youth athletes to raise their skill set. Elevate Your Game offers a fun and positive experience, customizable training programs at affordable prices backed by years of basketball playing and coaching experience. If this seems like the basketball experience you want for your youth player, please contact me.

Services Offered:

1. Shooting Training
2. Dribbling Training
3. Rebound Training
4. Passing Training
5. Defensive Training
6. Core Conditioning
7. Agility Training

Staff

Hi there, I am a former Baltimore Dream basketball player with 24 plus years of basketball coaching experience. I am a certified basketball coach that has coached at various levels for both boys and girls. I focus on improving and developing basketball fundamentals. I love helping youth athletes grow their game by focusing on building a strong fundamental foundation. My areas of expertise are shooting and dribbling but love coaching all aspects of the game. I am always excited to train players that have the will and heart it requires to play defense. Being a former basketball Director, I have evaluated and worked with over thousands of youth athletes and have even written training progression curriculums designed to help youth coaches improve. Currently I am a MIAA varsity assistant coach at Annapolis Area Christian Upper School. When I am not training players or coaching basketball, I am typically doing a variety of activities with my wife and three children. Want to work together? I’d love to hear from you.
