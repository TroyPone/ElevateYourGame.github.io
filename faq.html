Frequently Asked Questions

Are the training sessions customizable?

Yes each training session is customized based on the players skill set and area of focus in which they are willing to put the hard work and effort to turn their weaknesses into strengths.

What ages do you work with?

Our comprehensive training programs are meticulously designed to cater to a broad spectrum of young athletes, ranging from those as young as 7 years old up through collegiate-level players who are as old as 22 years of age.

How many players train together?

Training sessions are typically 1 on 1 sessions to maximize training repetition. In some cases clients have requested 2-3 player small group workouts which is price negotiable depending on the ages of the players. I recommend 1 on 1 sessions to maximize ball touches for the full 60 minute workout.

Where are the training sessions?

All 1 on 1 or small group training sessions are conducted at Elevate Your Game’s personal outdoor half-court basketball court.

When are the training sessions?

All training sessions are by appointment only. Please contact me for availability as I conduct training sessions nearly every day of the week weather permitting.

Is training equipment used and included during training sessions with no additional cost?

Yes, depending on the skill that is being worked on, all applicable training equipment will be provided and used during the training session at no additional charge.

Are contracts required per client?

Yes before training sessions begin, each client will be required to sign a customer service agreement and a release of liability waiver.

Do you train college basketball players?

Yes I do train college basketball players. I have trained JUCO, Division I, II and III athletes.

What makes Elevate Your Game different than other training companies?

I truly care about the progression and development of the youth athlete both physically and mentally. I love the sport of basketball at all levels and focus on small details to maximize results. I also enjoy sharing my knowledge of the game I love, with youth athletes that also have the same interest. Training sessions will keep the athletes attention and the level of intensity will increase as the players age and fundamental skill set increases.
